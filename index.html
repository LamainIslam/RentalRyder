More Than a Rental. We're Your Partner.

Why Riders Need to File Taxes

As a delivery rider, you're considered self-employed. This means you are responsible for registering with HMRC and filing an annual tax return. It can be confusing, but we make it simple.

Need help? Message us on WhatsApp

What RentalRyders Helps With

  • Self-employed registration
  • Year-end tax return filing
  • Reminders and live chat support

Our tax help service is included for all riders on our Standard Plan.

Built for Riders, by People Who Get It

We saw too many aspiring delivery riders struggling with the high costs of buying a moped and the confusing, stressful process of self-employed taxes. We knew there had to be a better way.

"Our mission is simple: To make transport and tax stress-free for delivery riders, so you can focus on what matters—earning."

Inspired by a highly successful and supportive rider community model in Portugal, we decided to bring the same all-in-one solution to the UK. RentalRyders was born to give every rider a fair and easy start.
